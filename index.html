Welcome to MHPS

Founded in 2018, the Mandya Homing Pigeon Society is dedicated to preserving and promoting the ancient sport of pigeon racing. celebrating the remarkable homing abilities of these incredible birds.

We organize regular races, training sessions, and educational programs to share our passion with the community and ensure the continuation of this traditional sport for future generations.
