Our Thanu Loft Story

From Humble Beginnings to Champion Breeders

Founded in 2017 by Giri Gowda V, Thanu Loft began with just a handful of pigeons in a small backyard loft. Today, we operate one of the most respected racing pigeon facilities in the region, known for our selective breeding program and commitment to excellence.

Our pigeons are bred for performance, health, and temperament. We combine traditional breeding techniques with modern training methods to produce birds that consistently outperform the competition.

Champion Bloodlines

Proven winners from top Indian stock

Advanced Training

Our birds undergo progressive distance training and orientation exercises to develop their homing instincts and stamina.

Optimal Health

Regular veterinary check-ups, balanced nutrition, and premium supplements ensure our pigeons are always at peak condition.

Selective Breeding

We meticulously track lineage and performance to pair only the strongest birds with the desired racing characteristics.
